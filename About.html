About Me

Hi, My name is Mahammad or Mo for short. I love to design, to make people happy with something I have created is something I strive for. I'm from Chorley however love to travel around Lancashire, Liverpool and Manchester. I've always had a passion about designing, It is something I've done since highschool. During highschool I did a lot of Photography, as a subject and a passion and from this I fell in love with designing through the use of Photoshop. As I continued my studies, I wanted to further develop my designing skills and I knew that having some compting knowledge would further enhance my skills and understanding. I had always known about illustrator and how it could enhance designing projects, for this reason after gaining some computing knowledge throughout college, I decided to pick it up. I was afraid at first, but the confidence I gained through studies and hard work helped overcome this. When it came to choosing a course at University, there where many options I could take within Design. Web Design was something I was interested in, as I enjoyed the projects done in College and it is something which could make use of many different designing applications and programs. I wanted to learn more about these, and decided to study Web Design & Developement. I learned many new skills, such as being able to understand the user, their design queries as well as how to prototype. I also learned many different coding skills focusing mainly on HTML, CSS and Javescript.
